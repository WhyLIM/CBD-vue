The data in our database is collected from a variety of sources, including peer-reviewed publications and direct user submissions. All data undergoes a curation process by our team to ensure accuracy and consistency before it is made public.

2. Can I download the data? ‚Äã

Yes. We provide options to download the entire database or a custom subset of the data in Excel or CSV format. Please visit the Download page for more information.

We welcome contributions from the research community. You can submit your data through our Submission page. Please use the provided Excel template to format your data correctly.

If you find any errors or have suggestions for improvement, please contact us. You can find our contact email on the Home page.

Our database is continuously growing. If you cannot find what you are looking for, it may not be in our database yet. We encourage you to check back regularly for updates or consider submitting the data yourself if you have it.
